Editorial Policy

What we publish

We publish practical tools and educational material about payroll calculations and record checks. Pages should solve a defined reader task, state assumptions and limitations, and avoid presenting generalized information as personalized professional advice.

Source and review standard

Material claims are checked against primary sources where available, including official provider documentation, pricing pages, contracts, government agencies, and regulators. Automated drafts are not eligible for publication until a human editorial review checks structure, usefulness, unsupported claims, and links.

Corrections and commercial independence

Affiliate relationships and advertising do not determine conclusions or rankings. When a material error is found, we correct the page and update its review date. Readers should verify time-sensitive information directly with the relevant provider or authority.

Professional-advice boundary

PayrollFixPro does not provide individualized legal, tax, accounting, insurance, medical, payroll, or financial advice. Calculators and checklists are planning aids, not substitutes for professional judgment.
