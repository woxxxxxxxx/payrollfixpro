About PayrollFixPro

PayrollFixPro is a free online resource offering 100+ payroll, salary, and tax calculators for employees, HR professionals, small business owners, and freelancers across the United States. Every tool is updated for the 2026 tax year and requires no account or signup.

Our calculators cover everything from basic salary conversion and paycheck estimation to advanced topics like quarterly estimated taxes, RSU vesting, 401k projections, and international salary comparisons. All calculations run entirely in your browser — your data is never stored or transmitted.

Whether you're negotiating a job offer, running small business payroll, or planning for retirement, PayrollFixPro has a free tool to help. Bookmark us and return whenever you need a quick, accurate calculation.
